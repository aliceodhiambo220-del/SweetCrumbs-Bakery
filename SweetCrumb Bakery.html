About us

founder in 2010 by Grace Camp & Samuel Kariuki with a single oven

and a dream to bring artisan baking to Nairobi.

Over the years , we have grown into a beloved neighbourhood bakery trusted by thousand of families , offices and event planning across the city. Our bakers rise before dawn every single day to ensure that what lands on your table is always fresh, always flavoured and always made with care

We believe baking is an art not a factory process.That is why we never use artificial preservatives and we source our flour,eggs and dairy from certifired local farms within 100km of Nairobi.
